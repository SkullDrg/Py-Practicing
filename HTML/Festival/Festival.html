Bands

DNCE

DNCE is an American dance-rock band consisting of lead singer Joe Jonas, drummer Jack Lawless, and guitarist JinJoo Lee. Bassist and keyboardist Cole Whittle was a part of the band from when it started in 2015 to when it went on hiatus in 2018. DNCE's music is mainly dance-rock, dance-pop, pop rock, and funk-pop.
Leo Santana

Nascido e criado no bairro do Lobato em Salvador, desde a adolescência sempre sonhou em trabalhar como músico, tanto que quando a família se reunia na casa dos avós na cidade de São Domingos, interior da Bahia, tinha o costume de dançar e cantar com os amigos.
Saulo Fernandes

Saulo Jorge Fernandes Navarro de Oliveira is a Brazilian Axé singer. Before becoming the lead singer of Banda Eva in March 2002, Saulo was the lead singer of the Banda Chica Fé. In 2013 he left Banda Eva to pursue a solo career
